Ink

Ink is my creative writing site. I use it to host the poems, short stories, and other creative writing pieces I've written that I want to share with the world.

Ink is built with Zola, a minimalist static site generator, and is intentionally kept simplistic and low-tech compared to my other sites. I still use my site-wide styles, but I have very little Ink-specific logic or styling. I did this to try to create a "vibe" less focused on tech and more focused on writing.

You might notice that many of the pieces have creation dates that predate this site by multiple years. This is intentional. I wrote those pieces long before creating this site, so when I moved them to this site, I gave them creation dates that were representative of when they were created rather than when they were published.
